Lasagna

Description

>

Popular Italian dish consisting of stacked layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, with meat, cheese, and marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Minced Garlic
  • Seasoning
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • No boil lasagna sheets

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°C
  2. Cook the ground beef with garlic and seasoning
  3. Mix cooked beef with the marinara sauce
  4. Make layers of lasagna sheets, meat, and Mozzarella cheese
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour
