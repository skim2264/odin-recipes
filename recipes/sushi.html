Sushi

Description

>

Japanese recipe of combining fresh raw salmon and seasoned sushi rice.

Ingredients

  • Sushi grade Salmon
  • Sushi rice
  • Sushi vinegar
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Cut your salmon into long 2cm thick slices
  2. Cook the sushi rice
  3. Combine the sushi rice and sugar in a small bowl
  4. Mix the above combination in the cooked sushi rice
  5. Roll small round balls of rice
  6. Place piece of salmon on each ball of rice
  7. Eat with soy sauce and wasabi. Enjoy!
